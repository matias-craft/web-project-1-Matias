Honda Civic

The Honda Civic is a line of cars manufactured by Honda. It is known for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and sporty design.

Specifications

  • Engine: 2.0L I4
  • Power: 158 hp (118 kW)
  • 0-60 mph: 7.5 seconds

Features

  • Honda Sensing safety suite
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Sporty exterior design
